***Question 1: "How likely is it that Bond will escape from Le Chiffre?"***
1. Not very likely
2. Somewhat likely
3. Slightly likely
4. Neutral or Uncertain
5. Moderately likely
6. Very likely
7. Extremely likely

Answer: 1. Not very likely

Bond and Vesper are the prisoners of Le Chiffre and his two gunmen, which suggests that their situation is precarious and escape is unlikely at this point.

***Question 2: "How suspenseful do you find this passage to be?"***

2. Somewhat suspenseful
3. Slightly suspenseful
4. Neutral or Uncertain
5. Moderately suspenseful
6. Very suspenseful
7. Extremely suspenseful

Answer: 6. Very suspenseful
