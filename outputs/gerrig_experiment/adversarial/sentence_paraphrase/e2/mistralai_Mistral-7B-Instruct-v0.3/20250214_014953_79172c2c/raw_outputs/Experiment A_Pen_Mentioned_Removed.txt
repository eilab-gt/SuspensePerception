 ***Question 1: "How likely is it that Bond will escape from Le Chiffre?"***
1. Not very likely
2. Somewhat likely
3. Slightly likely
4. Neutral or Uncertain
5. Moderately likely
6. Very likely
7. Extremely likely

Answer: 3. Slightly likely

Bond has shown agility and resourcefulness in the passage, but he is currently overpowered and disarmed by Le Chiffre's men. However, his attempt to escape and his continued attempts to hide his pen suggest that he may have a plan to escape.

***Question 2: "How suspenseful do you find this passage to be?"***
1. Not very suspenseful
2. Somewhat suspenseful
3. Slightly suspenseful
4. Neutral or Uncertain
5. Moderately suspenseful
6. Very suspenseful
7. Extremely suspenseful

Answer: 5. Moderately suspenseful

The passage describes a tense situation where Bond is a prisoner, and his attempts to escape add to the suspense. The reader is unsure if Bond will be able to escape or if Le Chiffre will discover his hidden pen.
