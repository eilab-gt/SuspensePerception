 ***Question 1: "How likely is it that Bond will escape from Le Chiffre?"***
1. Not very likely
2. Somewhat likely
3. Slightly likely
4. Neutral or Uncertain
5. Moderately likely
6. Very likely
7. Extremely likely

Answer: 3. Slightly likely

Bond has shown resourcefulness and agility in the passage, but he is currently overpowered and being searched for weapons. It is uncertain if he will be able to escape at this point.
