 ***Question 1: "How likely is it that Bond will escape from Le Chiffre?"***
1. Not very likely
2. Somewhat likely
3. Slightly likely
4. Neutral or Uncertain
5. Moderately likely
6. Very likely
7. Extremely likely

Answer: 3. Slightly likely

Bond has shown resourcefulness and agility in the passage, but he is currently overpowered and being searched for weapons. It suggests that he may have a chance to escape, but it is not guaranteed.

***Question 2: "How suspenseful do you find this passage to be?"***

3. Slightly suspenseful
4. Neutral or Uncertain
5. Moderately suspenseful
6. Very suspenseful
7. Extremely suspenseful

Answer: 5. Moderately suspenseful
